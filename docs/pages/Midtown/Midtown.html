Midtown Trolley

We are happy to announce the introduction of the Midtown Trolley! This trolley will improve walkability and reduce traffic along the Providence Rd corridor. The trolley will run every ten minutes so you won't have to wait.

We encourage all visitors visiting Midtown Charlotte by car to park in the designated park-and-ride garage off of Sharon Amity Rd and enjoy the free trolley service.

As Midtown Charlotte grows, we will be working to replace the trolley with the new Midtown Streetcar.
