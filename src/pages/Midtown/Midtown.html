Midtown Trolley

We are happy to announce the introduction of the Midtown Trolley! This trolley will improve walkability and reduce traffic along the Providence Rd corridor. The trolley will run every ten minutes so you won't have to wait.

We encourage all visitors visiting Midtown Charlotte by car to park in the designated park-and-ride garage near Sharon Amity Rd and enjoy the free trolley service.

Partners

  • Central Piedmont Community College (CPCC)
  • Novant Health Presbyterian Medical Center
  • Charlotte Area Transit System (CATS)

Future Streetcar

As Midtown grows, we will be working to replace the trolley with the new Midtown Streetcar.

To pay for this project, we need to secure financing from multiple sources:

  • Local businesses
  • City and neighborhood financing
  • Federal matching grants

To cover local obligations, we need to establish a Municipal Service District as prescribed under NC General Statutes, Chapter 160A, Article 23.
